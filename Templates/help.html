FAQ

How to add/remove stocks from portfolio or watch-list?

To add or remove stocks from the portfolio or watch-list navigate to the dashboard dropdown from the SIGHT APP top menu and choose either portfolio or watch-list. From there you will see two buttons under the Stocks graphic chart, use them to add or remove stocks from that list.

How to change personal information?

To edit your personal information navigate to the account page from the SIGHT APP top menu. From there you will be given the option to edit your accounts information.

How to reset password?

To reset your password navigate to the account page from the SIGHT APP top menu. From there you will be given the option to reset your password.

How to manage notifications alerts?

On the sites Dashboard dropdown menu you will find a section called "Settings". Navigate to this page to edit and control your apps settings.

Can I Buy/Sell Stocks in SIGHT APP?

No, SIGHT APP is NOT a trading application. It is only capable of tracking and analyzing stock market data to help Investor make more informed stock transactions.

Why can't I add stocks from other index such as Dow Jones Industrial Average?

SIGHT APP only analyzes and predicts the stocks values within the NASDAQ Index only. More support coming soon.
