About Us

SIGHT is a web app for stock market analysis and prediction using machine learning. The product allows a user to follow stock prices of his choosing to obtain a forecast of the stock prices. It will also monitor the stock they own and calculate projected profits or losses based on the amount of stock the user possesses. The product will not trade stock. Stock data will be presented in a time series chart. The goal of this product is to assist users in making decisions. The analysis and predictions are made by machine learning algorithms that processes the information retrieved from a database. This database is fed by a data mining algorithm. The factors taken into consideration for this algorithm will be historical stock value data and market closing time stock values.
